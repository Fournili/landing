8. Fees and Payment Structure

8.1 Fee Structure

Fournili charges a fixed fee of 5% on the total value of each successful order facilitated through the Platform. This fee is charged to the restaurant and calculated based on the each order that have been successfully delivered in a given month.

8.2 Collection and Payment

  • "Restaurant Payments:" Restaurants are required to pay the 5% fee on every successful orders at the end of each month. Fournili will generate an invoice detailing the total amount due based on the orders completed that month. Restaurants must pay the order price + %5 total fee to the Supplier after every successful delivery.
  • "Supplier Responsibility: " Suppliers are responsible for collecting the payment from restaurants, which includes the cost of the order and the additional 5% fee. Suppliers are required to remit Fournili's 5% fee of every successful delivery each month within one week (7 calendar days) .

8.3 Late Payments and Penalties

In the event that a supplier fails to remit the 5% fee to Fournili within the specified timeframe, or/and a Fast-Food/Restaurant refuses to pay the %5 fees, Fournili reserves the right to take legal action as per Algerian law. Late payments will incur additional charges, including interest, and may result in the suspension of the supplier's account until all dues are settled.

8.4 Payment Methods

For now , all payments must be made in cash . Fast-Foods/Restaurants are required to pay the 5% fee of every successful delivery in cash to the Supplier when invoiced by Fournili. Suppliers must also remit Fournili's 5% fee in cash within the specified period. Users are responsible for ensuring timely cash payments, and any delays may result in penalties or legal actions as outlined in these Terms.
